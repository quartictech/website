Our Products

We build SaaS products that manage your real time data and provide useful APIs and front ends.

Weyl

Weyl is the brain of our platform. It receives data and instructions from Jester that define super fast computations or logic. Weyl performs fast graph computations on the data in a way that abstracts data resilience and infrastructure issues. This means you can focus on writing business logic without worrying about configuring a computation cluster. Weyl also smartly handles which datasets it keeps in memory and which should be passed back and forth to disk, ensuring optimal performance for your use case, data scale and infrastructure.
