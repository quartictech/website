Our Technology

Our Platform

Cloud hosted software

All our software is deployed as a service and available on demand. We have a range of core components that let you manage your real time data as well as your custom business logic.

Additionally, we provide front ends to our platform that you can provide to your customers or use internally.

Using our software

Single users to entire companies

Our platform can be used by single users interested in performing a small analysis of their data as well as larger companies aiming to manage their real time data and interface with the rest of their infrastructure.

Our team will help you with any aspects of using our platform, from data ingestion to connecting outputs back to your physical infrastructure.
