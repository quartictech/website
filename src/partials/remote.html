Quartic Remote

Full situational awareness at your fingertips.

 

Quartic Remote enables field personnel to send and receive information and alerts to and from GroundControl.

  • Track locations and metrics in real time, viewable from GroundControl and by other field personnel. Collect the data for analysis.
  • Receive custom alerting based on any metrics from any data in the system. Reroute operatives with minimal effort, reducing cost and time.
  • Send data back to base via photos and custom forms. See this data integrated immediately alongside everything else in GroundControl.
