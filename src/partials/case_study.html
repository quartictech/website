Case study: highway maintenance.

Highway authorities in the UK are responsible for maintaining thousands of miles of paved carriageway and footway. Both reactive maintenance and proactive resurfacing are expensive, and mistakes cost time and money.
Quartic integrates existing asset databases and visual and digital inspection data, along with ad-hoc spreadsheets to present a unified picture of the network condition and maintenance schedule.
Our road-specific insights use all available data to accurately model deterioration and suggest optimal maintenance and treatment strategies. For example, we are able to spot opportunities for targeted patch resurfacing, leading to considerable cost savings.
