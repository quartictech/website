Quartic GroundControl

All your data managed, prepared and analysed in one platform. Receive and send real time updates to the field.

 

alerts and triggers

Create automated ‘set and forget’ alerts and triggers for tactical changes in the field. Warn your operatives they’ve moved out of a safe zone, or automatically reroute your deliveries based on new information.

analysis and visualisation

Analyse and visualise enterprise and field data. GroundControl provides an integrated Data Science platform as well as APIs for your existing analysis infrastructure.

live updates

Push complex information out to the field and receive updates from personnel in real time using Quartic Remote.

data integration

Integrate live and static data sources from across your organisation. Our flexible APIs are easy to use. Alternatively, our engineers will take care of this for you.

compute

Powerful analysis tools make it easy to dig into the data to identify trends and inefficiencies. Apply costing models to your operations. Match the movements of your personnel to physical assets.

your customers

Quartic Remote can be used as a customer facing phone app. Gather information and feedback from your customers, tie this into GroundControl and update your operations as needed.
