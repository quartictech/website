What it does

Our control platform and field app disseminate timely and useful information to field operatives and collect data straight from the field. All in real time.

 

collect

Collect, clean and manage all your enterprise data including from the field

eg, telemetry, databases, point-of-sale data

alert

Alert and notify your field and office personnel with important information

eg, update delivery routes as orders come in

optimise

Analyse, visualise, and eliminate inefficiencies across the enterprise

eg, optimise routes and staffing over time
