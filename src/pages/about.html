Meet Quartic.

Quartic Technologies was founded to build software and tools for organisations to collect, analyse and disseminate information throughout their organisation effortlessly.

 

 

Give everyone the information they need.

The last ten years of software and data management platforms have focused on tooling for developers and experts within the organisation. The frontline of an organisation's operations has been neglected.

We're changing this. Context and enterprise information, at your fingertips, for everyone.
