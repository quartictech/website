Meet Quartic.

Quartic Technologies was founded to build software and tools for organisations to collect, analyse, and disseminate information throughout their organisation effortlessly.

 

 

 

 

Give everyone the information they need.

The last ten years of software and data management platforms have focused on tooling for developers and experts within the organisation. The frontline of an organisation's operations has been neglected.

We're changing this. Context and enterprise information, at your fingertips, for everyone.

 

Our team.

Quartic was founded in 2016 by a group of software engineers and data scientists in London, UK. We've spent the last decade working on high performance computing, scientific data analysis, and enterprise software deployment.

 

Arlo Guneratne Bryer

CEO

Arlo runs operations and delivery. Before this, he ran several large enterprise software projects at Palantir Technologies. He holds a PhD in Physics from Imperial College London during which he worked on early analysis of LHC data at CERN.

Alex Sparrow
