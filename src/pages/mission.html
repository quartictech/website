Data done right.

Every device produces data.

Every organisation holds data.

Every decision is informed by data.

Data integration and management are the seminal problems of our era.

So humans spend their days curating, cleaning, joining, and cataloguing.

Quartic drives this effort to zero with state-of-the-art engineering and AI.

 

Our principles.

#1: Don’t repeat yourself.

Traditional products require armies of people repeating the same mundane data operations.

Whenever we see that, we eliminate it.

#2: Data pipelines are code.

We don’t pretend otherwise.

Our platform packages the best agile software-development techniques and makes them available to data-science/engineering teams.

#3: Build for the real world.

Our managed solution means that we’re our own customers.

This ensures our platform works for organisations that want practical answers, rather than over-engineered developer tools.

#4: Build for the future.

Our product makes use of state of the art AI and Machine Learning where possible.

It’s not magic. Yet.
