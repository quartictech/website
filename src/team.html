Our team

Arlo Guneratne Bryer - CEO

Arlo spent almost four years as a Deployment Strategist at Palantir, running and setting strategic direction on large commercial projects. He has led teams of engineers for customers in financial, insurance and pharmaceutical industries in addition to undertaking and guiding data analysis work. Arlo holds a PhD from Imperial College London, working at CERN analysing the first data from the Large Hadron Collider.

Alex Sparrow - CTO

Alex oversees all major technical decisions and guides our product direction. Prior to cofounding Quartic, he spent nearly four years as a Forward Deployed Software Engineer at Palantir, deploying products and building applications for processing and analysing data. Alex holds a PhD from Imperial College London, where he built high performance data analysis tools for scientists at CERN.

Oliver Charlesworth - Engineering Lead

Oliver spent three years as a Forward Deployed Software Engineer at Palantir, leading customer facing and product development projects, and seven years working on embedded signal processing and high performance computing at Cambridge Consultants and Maxeler Technologies. Oliver holds a Masters in Electronic Engineering from the University of York.
