How we work

We believe in using the right tools and ideas for the job.

Every organisation has unique data and unique problems.

We start by understanding and scoping the problem with you, be it a customer retention issue or understanding how your users interact with your platform.

From the initial problem, we'll work with you to identify relevant datasets, work with users to understand how they work and work with your organisation to ensure a smooth process. We then quickly build a first draft of a solution.

From our first cut solution, we enter a tight iteration loop between your business owners, users, and our engineers, taking feedback and using it to guide the products, analyses and deployment.

Finally, we deliver a reliable, effective solution to the problem whether that's a massive-scale bespoke software rollout across the enterprise, or an actionable data analysis for your business.
