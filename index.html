What we do

Custom Software

We have a strong track record at building reliable, powerful software to challenging deadlines. We specialise in software for data processing and analysis.

Data Analysis

We have extensive experience analysing real-world datasets - large and small. From data-driven visualisations to large-scale statistical and machine learning workflows, we can help you extract actionable intelligence from your data.

Client Implementation

Deploying complex software products in large organizations can be challenging. We are here to help. We can assist with every aspect of deployment, from project management to operations.

We believe in using the right tools and ideas for the job.

Every data analysis company in the world claims to have the right ‘data science’ solution for ‘big data’. In practice, there’s rarely a one-size fits all — each organisation has unique data and unique problems. These require unique, bespoke solutions building off state of the art techniques, software and infrastructure.

We start by understanding and scoping the problem with you, be it a customer retention issue or understanding how your users interact with your platform.

From the initial problem, we'll work with you to identify relevant datasets, work with users to sketch out what a solution might look like and quickly build a first draft of a solution.

From our first cut solution, we enter a tight iteration loop between your users and our engineers, taking feedback and using it to guide the features we build.
